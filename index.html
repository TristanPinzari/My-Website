Resume Parser

The hiring process for large companies is often a lengthy and costly process. Every year some FAANG companies recieve upward of 10M applications. Resume Parser allows applicants to upload their resume to the company's cloud storage. After applicants upload their resume, recruiters can look and search for resumes using keywords on the recruiter dashboard.

Made with: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Firebase

Twitter Clone

We wanted to create a service for friends, family, and coworkers to communicate and stay connected through the exchange of quick, frequent messages. People post Tweets, which may contain photos, videos, links, and text. These messages are posted to your profile, sent to your followers, and are searchable on Twitter search.

Made with: React, Tailwind CSS, Next.js, Next.auth, Firebase, HuggingFace NLP Model

Weather Aggregator

In 2023, The East Coast of the US was engulfed in smoke as a result of Canadian wildfires, triggering health alerts. Our project is a weather aggregator, whose purpose is to monitor weather in any given city to promote safety and awareness.

Made with: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, OpenWeather, NewsAPI
